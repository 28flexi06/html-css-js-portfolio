    Get To Know More

    Jazz & Soul Journey

    Because of her rich timbre and memorable emotional sound, Aleksandra makes her music unique and soulful. Her performance of "Je Suis Malade" by Alice Dona and Serge Lama amazed many listeners. Yuri Funahashi, pianist and chair of the Music Department at Colby College, said that "[Aleksandra’s] music moves her."
    Aleksandra is a winner and laureate of many International and National Jazz and Pop vocal competitions. Aleksandra got her classical basic musical education at the Children's Art School in choral art (alto) in Russia.
    Aleksandra studies Computer Science and Music at Colby College (ME, USA). She is the vocal soloist in "Gordon and the Centers,” a premier jazz group in Waterville that performs in restaurants and at various events organized by Colby College for delegations, faculty and students.
    Aleksandra adheres to Estill Voice technique . Unique voice and excellent technique allows her to work in various genres, including even Cuban and Puerto Rican folk percussive music. Her singing combines Western technique and folk motifs, uniting Russian folk music with Western style.
